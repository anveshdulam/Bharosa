## ⚙️ The 2-Minute Check-In Engine

The technical heart of the application is the **2-Minute Check-In Modal**. It is a dynamic, state-driven widget designed to bypass medical jargon and speak directly to human hesitation.

**The Workflow:**
1. **The Prompt:** *"What's holding you back?"*
2. **The Inputs:** User selects their core psychological barrier:
   * <kbd>😨 Fear of the result</kbd> 
   * <kbd>👁️ Fear of judgment</kbd>
   * <kbd>⏰ I don't have time</kbd>
   * <kbd>💰 Cost worries</kbd>
3. **The Output:** The engine processes the input and returns a deeply empathetic, customized response and a low-pressure next step (e.g., *"Read real stories"* or *"Start anonymous chat"*).

## 📈 Areas of Impact

Designed strictly aligned with the **DesignVerse 2026** problem statement criteria, Bharosa operates across four major dimensions:

* **📈 Early Detection:** Catch conditions before they become crises. Our check-ins flag risk factors and guide timely consultations.
* **🛡️ Preventive Care:** Shift from reactive to proactive health. Small, consistent nudges build long-term habits that prevent disease.
* **🧠 Mental Wellbeing:** Stigma around mental health is the highest barrier. Our non-judgmental platform creates a safe first conversation.
* **🤝 Community Health:** One person helped becomes a family protected. Bharosa scales trust across communities, not just individuals.

## 💻 Tech Stack & Architecture

This platform was built with a focus on smooth, "antigravity" physics, glassmorphism, and calming aesthetics to reduce user anxiety.

* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Styling & Layout:** Tailwind CSS
* **Interactivity & Animations:** Framer Motion (Spring physics, layout morphing, modal overlays)
* **Iconography:** Lucide React
* **Typography:** Fraunces (Serif) & Work Sans (Sans-Serif)
